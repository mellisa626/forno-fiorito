Our Story

Forno Fiorito started operations in November 2023, following the official opening of The Exchange TRX. The idea to open Forno Fiorito came from our love for authentic pizza cooked in a traditional oven. We wanted to create a place where customers can enjoy pizzas made with fresh, high-quality ingredients, paired with perfect flavors.

Origin of Forno Fiorito

"Forno" means oven, the heart of pizza-making, and "Fiorito" means blooming in Italian, symbolizing how our flavors blossom into something special. Each pizza at Forno Fiorito is crafted with passion, fresh ingredients, and unique combinations that create a delightful taste experience.

  • Forno Fiorito started operations in November 2023, following the official opening of The Exchange TRX.

  • The idea to open "Forno Fiorito": Started from our love for authentic pizza cooked in a traditional oven. We wanted to create a place where customers can enjoy pizzas made with fresh, high-quality ingredients, paired with perfect flavors.

  • Oven as the Main Focus: This name highlights the magic of the traditional oven, which not only cooks but also adds warmth and flavor to every pizza. Each pizza comes out with perfect texture and taste, representing the true tradition of pizza-making.

  • Creativity in Pizza: This name also reflects creativity in crafting pizzas with unique toppings that are not only visually appealing but also bring rich and fresh flavors. Each pizza is made with great attention, resulting in a blossoming and delicious combination of tastes, making it more than just a dish – it’s a refreshing visual and flavorful experience.

  • Refreshing beverages: The beverages at Forno Fiorito are all made fresh and carefully selected to complement our pizza dishes. Each drink is crafted to pair perfectly with the flavors of our pizzas, providing a balanced dining experience without overpowering the unique taste of the pizza.
